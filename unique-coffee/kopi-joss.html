Kopi Joss

Kopi Joss is an exciting Indonesian coffee that takes a dramatic twist with the addition of a burning piece of charcoal. This method adds a smoky flavor to the brew while also reducing acidity, creating a distinct taste profile. Kopi Joss offers a robust coffee experience with a rich, smoky undertone. The addition of charcoal provides a unique depth of flavor, making each cup a memorable experience that stands out from typical coffee offerings.

Originating in Yogyakarta, Kopi Joss is often prepared by street vendors, who have turned the process into a theatrical experience. The drink has roots in local traditions, where coffee is not just a beverage but a part of social gatherings. Kopi Joss reflects the vibrant street culture of Indonesia, where coffee serves as a communal drink that brings people together. The preparation is often accompanied by lively conversations and the sounds of bustling street life.

How it’s Made

To make Kopi Joss, strong coffee is brewed using traditional methods, often with a dark roast. Once brewed, a piece of glowing charcoal is dropped into the cup. This not only infuses the coffee with a smoky flavor but also helps to settle any grounds at the bottom.
