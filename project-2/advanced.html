Advanced

Dive Deeper Depths

Dive enthusiasts, whether freshly certified with PADI Open Water or boasting experience from other agencies, rejoice! The PADI Advanced Open Water Course is your ticket to heightened confidence, refined skills, and unparalleled underwater excitement.

  • Certified Open Water Diver/Junior Open Water Diver (or qualifying certification)
  • Minimum age: 12 years or older
  • Completion of eLearning modules (6-8 hours)
  • Attendance at five Adventure Dives:
    • Deep Dive
    • Navigation Dive
    • Three Elective Dives

HIGHLIGHTS

Explore New Depths

The Deep Adventure Dive takes you to 30 meters, opening up new dive sites and opportunities to witness larger marine life.

Navigate with Precision

Enhance your underwater navigation skills, moving beyond basic compass use to natural references, depths, distances, and navigation plots.

Master Buoyancy

The Peak Performance Buoyancy Dive refines your buoyancy control, making you more efficient underwater and a responsible guardian of marine environments.

Think Like a Diver

Develop dive skills centered around planning with secondary objectives, situational awareness, effective task loading, and forming good dive habits.

Embrace Dive Computer Expertise Fully

Learn the ins and outs of dive computers, indispensable tools for monitoring ascent rates, calculating no stop time, and enhancing overall safety.
