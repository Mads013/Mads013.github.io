Discover Scuba

Dive and Explore

Dive into the extraordinary world of scuba with our captivating Discover Scuba Diving sessions! Immerse yourself in an unforgettable experience that begins with an engaging video presentation and a brief knowledge session at our dive shop. From there, you'll take your first underwater breaths in the comfort of a pool, fully equipped with scuba gear.

Minimum Age is 10 years old.

HIGHLIGHTS

If you have a group of 5 or more, contact us to arrange a specially scheduled session just for your crew.

Ensure a seamless dive by completing the Discover Scuba Diving Brochure and required Medical Statements in advance. Safety is our priority, so if you answer yes to any medical questions, consult your physician and have them sign on Page 2 before your in-water session.
