Open Water

Dive into the Unknown

Embark on a mesmerizing underwater journey with the globally acclaimed PADI Open Water Diver course. Whether you're enchanted by the ocean's allure or captivated by the magic of water, this course is your gateway to a world of scuba exploration. Forge new bonds, master the essentials of diving, and unlock a lifetime of certified underwater adventures..

HIGHLIGHTS

Integrated Training:

The PADI Open Water Diver course integrates water skills assessment, knowledge development, and confined water practice, leading to the pinnacle â€“ four open water dives.

Hands-On Application:

The four dives are a hands-on application of your training, boosting your confidence and competence in a real underwater setting.

Versatile Skills:

Dive-flexible skills, from cramp removal to controlled emergency swimming ascents, are introduced in various scenarios, preparing you for diverse diving challenges.

Depth Progression:

Explore depths up to 12 meters/40 feet in dives 1 and 2, then push further to a maximum of 18 meters/60 feet in dives 3 and 4, ensuring a thrilling progression.

Certification Milestone:

Completion of the four open water dives earns you the PADI Open Water Diver certification, unlocking opportunities at dive centers, resorts, and liveaboards worldwide. Your underwater adventure begins!
