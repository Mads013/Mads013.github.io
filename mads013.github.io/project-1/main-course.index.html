Lemon Herb & Chicken

class="ingredient-column">

    Ingridients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, zest and juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves (or 1 teaspoon dried thyme)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried rosemary)
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: Lemon slices and additional fresh herbs for garnish

This Lemon Herb Chicken recipe offers tender, juicy chicken breasts infused with the vibrant flavors of fresh lemon zest, aromatic garlic, and a blend of fragrant herbs like parsley, thyme, and rosemary. Searing the chicken to a golden perfection before a brief bake in the oven results in a delightful balance of citrusy zing and savory herb notes. The aromatic marinade, drizzled over the cooked chicken, elevates this dish into a delightful and refreshing culinary experience, making it an ideal choice for a quick and flavorful meal.

Recipe

Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped parsley, thyme, rosemary, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and black pepper to create the marinade. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish, and pour the marinade over them. Ensure the chicken is well coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse. Preheat the Oven: Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C). Sear the Chicken: Heat an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add a bit of olive oil to the skillet. Remove the chicken from the marinade (save the marinade for later) and sear each side for 2-3 minutes, until golden brown. Bake the Chicken: Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C). Deglaze and Serve: While the chicken is baking, pour the reserved marinade into a small saucepan. Bring it to a boil and then reduce the heat, letting it simmer for a few minutes. Once the chicken is done, drizzle the cooked marinade over the chicken. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with lemon slices and fresh herbs if desired. Serve the Lemon Herb Chicken with your choice of sides, such as steamed vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad.
